Explanations:

1. C1 continuity means both the function and its first derivative are continuous.

2. This is the standard implicit equation for a circle with center q and radius r.

3. Cubic Bézier curves require exactly 4 control points.

4. Hermite curves are defined by endpoints and their slopes.

5. B-splines provide G1 continuity between segments.

6. Parametric form can involve expensive trigonometric calculations.

7. Only the first and last control points are interpolated.

8. Each curve type has distinct characteristics in how it's defined and controlled.

9. de Casteljau's algorithm is a numerical method for evaluating Bézier curves.

10. Cubic polynomials are needed to satisfy all continuity constraints.
