3. When lighting is enabled, material properties are used instead of direct colors.

4. Double buffering prevents screen tearing and flickering during rendering.

5. The modelview matrix combines model and view transformations.

6. Column-major order is the standard matrix storage format in OpenGL.

7. This ensures a clean slate for new transformations.
