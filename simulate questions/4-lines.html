Answer Key:

Multiple Choice Questions:

1. b - They are perpendicular

2. c - A parameter that can be thought of as time

3. b - The distances d and D

True/False Questions:

4. True

5. True

Matching Question:

Dot Product → Finding vector length

Parametric Form → Describing a moving point

Implicit Form → Representing vertical lines

Explanations:

1. When two vectors are perpendicular (normal to each other), their dot product is zero.

2. In parametric form, t represents a parameter that can be thought of as time, allowing us to describe any point on the line.

3. When using similar triangles to find line intersections, we need both d and D to determine the intersection point.

4. The implicit form equation is precisely the mathematical definition of points on a line.

5. The sign (positive or negative) of the distance indicates which side of the line the point lies on.

6. The matching reflects the primary applications of each concept as discussed in the lecture.
